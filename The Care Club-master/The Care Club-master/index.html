About

Learn more about us

The care club is a non-profit foundation formed by college students of New Horizon College Of Engineering.The main purpose of this club is to connect with students all around the country to look and work on the social issues and concerns.Website on detail information of the care club foundation and also the events and activities conducted by the club. Donation made by the students for the social work. Students can apply for different roles like volunteering, campus ambassadors and social marketing by providing their details in the website.

The Care Club intends to widen this exercise of identification. It is not just limited to a specific gender, age group or a particular methodology for conditioning the advancement of this society. Whether it is about women, children, older people, it tries to reach out to everybody and every section of society.Our Vision is to help everyone in creating their own ‘Pahchan’-identity and live a life with dignity. We believe in urgent action, innovation, and the necessity of transformation—within the world and our own organization. We seek a world of hope, tolerance and social justice, where poverty has been overcome and all people live with dignity and security.
